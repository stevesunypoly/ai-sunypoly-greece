## For Students: Teach Yourself

You do not need to wait for a course. You need two files and access to any free AI model.

### Step 1 — Download two files

| File | What it is |
|------|------------|
| [`LP.html`](LP.html) | The AI Literacy Learner's Permit — a 15-exercise curriculum for any topic, any discipline |
| [`starter-prompt.txt`](starter-prompt.txt) | The prompt you paste into your model to get started |

### Step 2 — Open any free AI model

Claude · ChatGPT · Gemini — free tier is sufficient.

### Step 3 — Upload both files and paste the prompt

The starter prompt tells the model to guide you through the curriculum one exercise at a time, evaluate your work, and advance you when you're ready.

**Your topic is yours to choose.** Data science, machine learning, cybersecurity, or anything you're genuinely curious about. The curriculum works for any subject.

---

## The Curriculum: Learner's Permit

The Learner's Permit is a 15-exercise, content-neutral scaffold for generative AI literacy. It is organized in three phases:

- **Phase I — Learning to Drive** (Exercises 1–5): You and the model. Learn the instrument.
- **Phase II — Building Skill** (Exercises 6–10): Multi-model work, reading with AI, building your workbench.
- **Phase III — Independent Operation** (Exercises 11–15): Produce real documents. Own the output.

Estimated time: ~5 hours total. You do not need to finish in one session.
